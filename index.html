// 01 — Launch

The Project

Project Idea

Automated Cinema Slider

A programmable motorized camera slider controlled by Arduino. The user sets movement speed, direction, and travel duration via physical controls — the system then executes smooth, repeatable camera movements ideal for cinematic video production. Designed as a real, usable filmmaking tool rather than a demonstration prototype.

★ What makes it unique: Bridges embedded systems with professional filmmaking — a programmable creative tool, not just a reactive robot.

Research

Similar Projects

Reviewed the Edelkrone SliderPLUS (commercial motorized slider, $400+), open-source Arduino CNC-based sliders on Instructables, and DIY DSLR dolly builds using stepper motors and GT2 belt systems. Our version is optimized for affordability, classroom-buildable components, and direct creative utility.

Hardware List
